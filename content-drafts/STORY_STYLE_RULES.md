# NT2 Verhalenmaker story style rules

- Use natural Dutch at CEFR B1 level, except for the selected target words.
- Keep each story page at exactly four sentences with two target words.
- Mix short and longer sentences, but avoid a fixed pattern across pages.
- Do not let one connector dominate a chapter. Vary simple B1 connectors such as `want`, `dus`, `daarom`, `terwijl`, `als`, `wanneer`, `zodat`, `waardoor`, `daardoor`, `maar`, `toch` and occasional `omdat`.
- Every page should read like a small moment from daily life in the Netherlands, not like a grammar exercise.
- After text edits, regenerate the matching story audio before publishing.
