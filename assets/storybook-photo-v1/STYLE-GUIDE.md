# Storybook Image Style Guide

Reference image: `assets/reference/sample-pic-page-1-image-1.jpg`

Use this style for the NT2 storybook image pack:

- Photorealistic Instagram lifestyle photography, not illustration.
- Groningen must be recognizable when relevant: Martinitoren, red-brick streets, canals, bicycles, Dutch storefronts, student-city atmosphere.
- A new immigrant protagonist must be visible in the scene, preferably waist-up or full-body, with natural expression and clear headroom.
- Character continuity means the same general person/age/face and immigrant learning journey, not the same clothes every day.
- Vary wardrobe, hairstyle, accessories, body pose, camera angle, season/time of day, and distance from camera across pages.
- Vary face direction deliberately. Across each 5-page story, avoid mostly right-side three-quarter portraits. Include a balanced mix of frontal view, left-side profile, right-side profile, over-the-shoulder/back view, wide environmental view, and close action detail.
- A good 5-page storybook set should feel like this kind of sequence: page 1 establishing/wide, page 2 left-profile or side action, page 3 over-the-shoulder or hands-at-work, page 4 frontal conversation, page 5 wider closing moment.
- Avoid repeating the same denim jacket/crossbody-bag look more than once within a story. Use realistic changes such as sweater, blouse, raincoat, apron, work vest, cardigan, scarf, hair tied back, loose hair, or casual coat.
- Vary Groningen locations strongly: canal street, apartment entrance, community center, classroom, archive, old restaurant, park, office, workshop, care workplace, kitchen, logistics warehouse, port area, market, bus stop, and bicycle route.
- Every story should feel like it takes place across several days or moments of life, not one long photo shoot.
- The image must show a concrete action from the page text: studying, volunteering, visiting UWV, meeting neighbors, working, cooking, cycling, walking in the city, or caring for nature.
- Warm daylight, natural colors, shallow-to-medium depth of field, clean composition.
- No text overlays, no UI, no posters, no random landscape-only photos, no cropped-off head.

Prompt skeleton:

```text
Instagram-style photorealistic lifestyle photo in Groningen, the Netherlands.
Recognizable local context: <specific Groningen place or environment>.
Main subject: a friendly new immigrant NT2 learner, recognizable from the story but with varied outfit/hair/pose for this moment, visible in the foreground, natural expression, fully in frame with generous headroom.
Concrete action: <exact action from this story page>.
Variety note: <specific different clothing, hair, camera angle, and background for this page>.
Composition note: <specific face direction and framing, e.g. left profile, frontal, over-the-shoulder, low wide shot, hands/action detail>.
Mood and lighting: warm natural daylight, candid documentary composition, realistic high-end mobile photography.
No text, no logo, no watermark, no cartoon, no empty landscape.
```
